Box Book, 2018

book board, drawing paper, PVA glue, book cloth


This book was created with by printing out pen and pencil drawn sketches on both sides of a piece of paper. Each section of the book pops up to create an open box. When all boxes are open, they will refold individually when the user accordions the book back to its folded state.
